Where does my money go?

Our team is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations go towards maintaining and improving the Hudson Hybrids Robotics team. More specifically, donations can be used to purchase:

  • Robot parts
  • Tools
  • Supplies for constructing practice competition field elements
  • Supplies for our "pit" at competitions (tool storage units, tables, etc.)
  • Community outreach

Running an FRC team is expensive. On top of funding needed to construct our robot, annual registration costs our team $5,000. Therefore, your support is vital to the success of our team, and we are thankful for all that you can contribute.
